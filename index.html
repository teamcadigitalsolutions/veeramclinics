Pulmonologists focus on diagnosing and treating respiratory system diseases, including asthma, COPD, and lung infections.

Orthopedics

Orthopedics deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of muscule system disorders, including bones, joints, ligaments, and tendons.
