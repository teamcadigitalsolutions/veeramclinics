who we are

At Veeram Clinics, we are dedicated to delivering compassionate, comprehensive healthcare with a personal touch. With a team of experienced doctors, advanced diagnostic tools, and a patient-first approach, we strive to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone. Whether it's preventive care, diagnostics, or treatment, we are here to support your journey to better health — every step of the way.

our missions

  • To provide high-quality, affordable healthcare services for all
  • To promote preventive care and patient education
  • To use the latest medical technology for accurate diagnosis
  • To create a welcoming and supportive environment for patients
  • To ensure timely, effective, and ethical medical care
  • To continually evolve with innovations in healthcare
  • To build lasting relationships based on trust and care

Professional Doctor

Our professional doctors are highly qualified and experienced in providing compassionate, evidence-based care tailored to each patient. With a focus on your well-being, we ensure every consultation is thorough and patient-centered.

  • Certified and experienced medical staff
  • Personalized diagnosis and treatment plans
  • Compassionate and attentive patient care
  • Ongoing training in the latest medical advancements
  • Focus on long-term health and prevention

General Checkup

Regular general checkups are key to maintaining overall health and detecting potential issues early. Our healthcare professionals provide comprehensive assessments to ensure your well-being and guide you toward a healthier lifestyle.

  • Routine physical examinations
  • Blood pressure and vital signs monitoring
  • Health risk assessments and screenings
  • Personalized lifestyle and wellness advice
  • Early detection and prevention of illness

Emergency Service

Our emergency services are available 24/7 to handle urgent medical situations with speed and care. Staffed by trained professionals and equipped with advanced facilities, we ensure immediate response and critical support when you need it most.

  • 24/7 availability for medical emergencies
  • Fully equipped emergency response units
  • Experienced doctors and paramedics on call
  • Rapid diagnosis and stabilization of patients
  • Seamless coordination with other departments for specialized care

Blood Tests & ECG

Our laboratory and cardiology diagnostics offer precise and timely blood tests and ECG services to support early detection and effective treatment planning. Our experienced team ensures fast and reliable results using state-of-the-art equipment.

  • Comprehensive blood testing for accurate diagnosis
  • Routine and specialized pathology services
  • Electrocardiograms (ECG) for heart health analysis
  • Minimal wait times with same-day reporting
  • Performed by certified technicians and medical staff

Daycare & Vaccination

Our Daycare & Vaccination services are designed to provide children with a safe, nurturing environment and essential immunizations to protect their health. We follow recommended schedules and offer professional care to support every child’s development.

  • Routine childhood and adult vaccinations
  • Daycare services in a hygienic and monitored environment
  • Skilled pediatric and nursing staff
  • Educational support and developmental activities
  • Parental guidance and post-vaccination care

Inhouse Pharmacy

Our inhouse pharmacy provides patients with convenient access to a full range of prescription and over-the-counter medications. With professional pharmacists on-site, we ensure that all medications are dispensed accurately and that patients receive proper guidance for their use.

  • Wide selection of prescription and OTC medications
  • On-site licensed pharmacists for consultation
  • Quick and easy access right after your appointment
  • Medication guidance and dosage instructions
  • Affordable pricing and insurance coordination
